ggplot(data = mpg) +
  geom_point(mapping = aes(x = displ, y = hwy))

  • Overall: Yup, cars with bigger engine displacement (displ) get lower fuel efficiency (hwy). See ?mpg for definitions.

  • Note: While mapping is often omitted, it is instructive to think of aes() as a mapping of variables to different aesthetics.
